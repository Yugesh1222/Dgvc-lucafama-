Who We Are

Lucafama is the premier student forum at DG Vaishnav College, dedicated to fostering student engagement, organizing cultural and academic events, and providing a platform for students to showcase their talents and skills.

Established with the vision of creating a vibrant campus community, Lucafama has been at the forefront of organizing inter-collegiate competitions, workshops, seminars, and cultural festivals.

About DG Vaishnav College

DG Vaishnav College, established in 1964, is one of the premier institutions in Chennai. It offers UG & PG programs in Arts, Science, and Commerce, with a strong focus on holistic student development.
